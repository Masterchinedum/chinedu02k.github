2020/2021 Registration Guidelines

Guidelines for Registration for Fresh and Returning Students

  1. (Fresh Students Only) Complete Fresh Student Biodata, Medical, Sport and Other Details form: it will require you uploading the scanned copy (colored) of the following documents
    • O'Level Certificate/Statement of result
    • Birth Certificate/Sworn Affidavit
    • Attestation Letter
    • Signed Declaration of Good Conduct (download copy here)
    • UTME Admission Letter
    • UTME Result
    • ND/NCE/A'Level Certificate or Statement of Result (DE Only)
  2. Check your email for Student portal username & Password for login
  3. Retrieve your FUNAAB email account to grant you access to G Suite for Education.
  4. Login to Generate school fee invoice
  5. Pay invoice online or via other channels
  6. Generate School Fee Receipt
  7. Register for Courses
  8. Pay Student Union Dues
  9. Bidding for Hostel to be announced later.
  10. (Fresh Students Only) Login to upload Signed Matriculation Oath

ATTN: Final Year Students - NYSC Mobilistation Exercise

In preparation towards a seamless NYSC Pre-mobilisation exercise for Prospective Corps Members (PCMs), the Students Affairs Division wishes to inform all Final Year Students to kindly submit their JAMB Admission Letter to the Students Affairs Division in room B119 and B127 on or before 17th September, 2021

Students who had changed their course offered by JAMB should indicate at the point of submission. While those with initials are also expected to write their names in full in the proper order i.e. SURNAME, FIRST and OTHER NAME at the point of submission.

This is aim at eradicating errors during the mobilisation exercise.

Your compliances to the above directive would be greatly appreciated.

Thank you.

Prof. A. M. Omemu.

Dean, Student Affairs.
